Good to know

Frequently asked questions

Where is Mint Dentistry located in Pune?
We have two clinics. Kothrud: 202 B Shree Datta Krupa Building, Late GA Kulkarni Path, opposite Karishma Society, Kothrud, Pune 411038. Sadashiv Peth: Shop No. 2, 1754, Vishnusadashiv Building, opposite Scout Ground, Sadashiv Peth, Pune 411030.
Is root canal treatment painful?
Not at Mint Dentistry. Root canals are performed by our endodontist Dr. Krati Jain under modern local anaesthesia, so treatment is comfortable and largely painless — and many cases are finished in a single sitting.
Do you treat children?
Yes. Dr. Dipti Gholap is an MDS paediatric (Paedodontics) specialist who looks after children in a calm, fear-free environment — from first check-ups to preventive care and treating cavities.
How do I book an appointment?
Just call or WhatsApp the clinic nearest you — Kothrud on 90051 68008 or Sadashiv Peth on 77096 86905 — and our team will find a time that suits you. Both clinics are open Monday to Saturday, 10am–8pm, and closed on Sundays.
What treatments do you offer?
Cosmetic dentistry (ceramic veneers and smile design), ceramic crowns and bridges, tooth-coloured fillings, painless root canal treatment, routine check-ups, scaling and polishing, and paediatric dental care.

Ready for a healthier, happier smile?

Book with the Mint Dentistry clinic nearest you. We'll take the time to listen, explain your options clearly and look after you gently.
